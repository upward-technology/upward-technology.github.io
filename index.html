Introduction

Upward Technology, LLC is a women and minority-owned small business based in Washington D.C. metro area. Customer success is our sole mission, and we dedicate our efforts to provide high-quality Telecom and software development services to our customers.

  • Capability Statement

Telecom Service

  • Network Operation

    Network is critical infrastructure, but it is often very complex. Upward Technology helps our customers to manage their networks more securely and efficiently. Our network operation service has a broad coverage including network monitoring, troubleshooting, automation, modeling and simulations.

  • Network Data Analysis

    Discovering business intelligence from massive network data can be very challenging. Upward Technology provides excellent data analysis service such as streamlining the ETL process, data cleaning, formation, visualization, and machine learning.

  • Network Security

    With the wide-spread computer virus, malware and ransomware, businesses are at risk all the time if not adequately addressed. Upward Technology helps our customers to build the first line of defense by hardening the network perimeter, including security monitoring, intrusion detection, and customized network security software development.

Software Development

Upward Technology owns over 40 years of combined experiences on software development. Our service covers the full software development life cycle, from customer requirements collection, the system design and implementation, to the product deployment and maintenance.

  • Web Application Development: Upward Technology uses the latest technology to help customers to build interactive and responsive web applications. Through our intelligent data-driven web applications, customers can better streamline their business operations and make more informed decisions.
  • Open Source Software Development: Our experienced team of engineers specializes in developing customized applications using open source software. We can build custom solutions designed around your performance or security requirements.
